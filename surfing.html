Surfing

Surfing is a water sport where athletes ride waves on surfboards, using balance and skill to glide across the oceans surface. Originating in Polynesia and popularized in Hawaii, it has become a global pastime and lifestyle symbolizing freedom and connection to nature. Surfers rely on reading wave patterns, timing, and physical endurance, making it both an art and a sport.
